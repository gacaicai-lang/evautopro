    Frequently Asked Questions

    How does Grand Coatings compare to imported European brands like Hempel, Jotun, or AkzoNobel?

    Comparable performance verified by ISO 12944, NACE SP0108, and API third-party testing — at 30-50% lower price point. Lead time 3-4 weeks vs 8-12 weeks for European imports. Full TDS, MSDS, and independent test reports issued with every delivery.

    What is the typical lead time and minimum order quantity?

    Standard formulations: 3-4 weeks production plus shipping. Custom OEM colors: 5-6 weeks. MOQ: 1 ton for in-stock systems, 5 tons for custom formulations. Dock-to-dock CIF / FOB / EXW available to all major export markets.

    Can you provide on-site application support?

    Yes. AMPP-equivalent qualified technicians available for pre-job inspections, surface preparation audits, applicator training, and DFT verification. Travel to Vietnam, India, Russia, Indonesia is routine.

    Do you support OEM and private-label packaging?

    Yes. Private-label packaging — your brand on cans, labels, shipping cartons — available at MOQ 10 tons. Includes custom color matching, custom TDS document with your branding, and OEM master pack configurations.

    What standards are your products certified to?

    ISO 12944-5/-9, NACE SP0108/SP0198, API 650/2218, SSPC-Paint 16/20, IMO PSPC, NORSOK M-501, UL 1709, GB/T 30790, HG/T 3668, NSF 61, FDA 21 CFR 175.300. Independent third-party test reports issued with each Technical Data Sheet on request.
