Description

Key Highlights:
  • Impressive Range: Choose between a 420 km or an extended 510 km (CLTC) range, catering to diverse urban and suburban driving needs.
  • Efficient Performance: Powered by a 100 kW motor delivering 180 N·m of torque, achieving 0-100 km/h in just 7.3 seconds for responsive city driving.
  • Rapid Recharging: Utilizes advanced BYD Blade Battery technology, supporting DC fast charging from 30% to 80% in approximately 30 minutes.
  • Optimized Efficiency: Maintains low energy consumption at 16 kWh/100 km, maximizing operational cost savings for end-users.

Design & Tech:
The Qin Plus EV features a sleek, modern 4-door sedan design with a 2718mm wheelbase for spacious interior comfort. It integrates BYD's cutting-edge Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) Blade Battery, renowned for its superior safety and longevity. The cabin is designed with a focus on driver ergonomics and contemporary connectivity features.
