Whether for B2B bulk orders, dealer partnerships, or individual purchases, MashangAuto aims to be a reliable bridge connecting global buyers to high-quality Chinese automotive products and services.

How to ensure that the vehicle purchased from a Chinese car dealer is legal?

To ensure the legality of vehicles bought from a Chinese car dealer, focus on three key points. First, verify the dealer’s qualifications—confirm they hold legal authorization to sell Chinese – brand vehicles. Second, check the vehicle’s documents thoroughly to ensure essentials like import certificates, certificates of conformity, and insurance are complete and valid. Third, understand your local laws, especially the regulations on importing and selling Chinese cars in your country. You can also contact us directly; we’ll assist you with these checks to ensure everything is in order.

After purchasing a car, how long does it usually take to go through the export procedures?
After you purchase a vehicle, we will handle a full set of export procedures including vehicle inspection, export license application, and customs declaration. Under normal circumstances, this process takes about 15 to 20 working days.
Specifically, the vehicle will first undergo a professional inspection to ensure it meets export standards. Local inspection for ordinary vehicles can be completed quickly, while inspections with CMA-qualified institutions will be arranged promptly if needed. Next, we apply for the export license through the “Unified Platform of Ministry of Commerce Business Systems”. With complete materials (such as sales contracts and inspection reports), the license is usually approved within 1 to 3 working days. Finally, we complete customs declaration via the “Single Window” system—thanks to paperless verification of electronic licenses, customs clearance can be finalized in just hours in optimized ports like Dalian.
The overall timeline is relatively stable, though it may be slightly adjusted based on factors like vehicle type (new vs. used cars) and whether additional preparations (e.g., vehicle conditioning) are required. We will keep you updated on the progress at each stage to ensure smooth procedures.

  • Original Spare Parts: We supply genuine original spare parts for all models we sell, ensuring consistency with the vehicle’s original configuration and maintaining repair quality.

3. Vehicle Inspection & Evaluation

  • Vehicle Health Check: We provide comprehensive vehicle health checks, including diagnostic tests for power systems (batteries for EVs, engines for fuel cars), safety components, and electronic controls. Detailed reports help customers understand the vehicle’s current status and potential issues.
  • Used Car Evaluation: For used cars we acquire, we conduct professional evaluations based on market conditions, vehicle age, mileage, and condition. This ensures fair and reasonable pricing for each used car.

4. Export Procedure Handling

We take charge of all paperwork and formalities required to export vehicles from China, including vehicle inspection, export license application, and customs declaration. This saves customers the hassle of navigating complex cross-border procedures.

5. After-Sales Service

  • Warranty & Quality Assurance: For customers looking to become local dealers, we can customize warranty and quality assurance plans (such as 1-year/20,000km basic warranties or extended coverage for key components) through consultations with our business managers.
  • After-Sales Repair Center Construction: We offer tailored solutions for building local after-sales repair centers, including site planning (workshop layout, equipment placement), qualification guidance (local industry certification application), and standardized service process design.
  • Equipment Provision: We supply professional repair equipment matching Chinese vehicle models, such as EV battery diagnostic instruments, chassis alignment machines, and engine testing tools—all from certified manufacturers to ensure repair accuracy.
  • Engineer Training: We provide online and offline training for repair engineers: online courses cover basic vehicle maintenance knowledge and model-specific repair tutorials (updated quarterly), while offline hands-on training (held at our China-based training center or on-site locally) focuses on practical operations like EV battery replacement and intelligent system debugging.

6. Customer Care & Consultation

  • Personalized Consultation: Our sales team offers one-on-one personalized consulting, helping customers select suitable vehicles and solutions. We provide in-depth support for customers planning to sell Chinese cars locally, including market demand analysis and B2B cooperation model recommendations (e.g., bulk purchasing, regional agency).
  • Customer Care: We value every customer feedback and have a dedicated customer service team that responds to inquiries within 4 working hours. We also conduct regular follow-ups (post-purchase, post-export) to continuously improve service quality and customer satisfaction.
What are the advantages of buying a car from a Chinese car dealer?

Buying a car from a Chinese car dealer comes with three key advantages that align with diverse buyer needs. First, there’s strong price competitiveness—thanks to China’s mature automotive supply chain, Chinese cars (especially electric and hybrid models) often deliver better performance and configuration at a more affordable price compared to counterparts in the same segment, offering excellent cost-effectiveness. Second, you’ll get diverse choices: whether you’re looking for a budget-friendly compact electric car for daily commuting, a high-end intelligent sedan for business use, or a heavy-duty commercial vehicle for work, Chinese brands cover multiple market segments to match different budgets and usage scenarios. Third, there’s leading-edge technology support: Chinese car dealers focus on advanced fields like new energy and smart driving—many models come with long-range battery systems, fast-charging technology, and intelligent cockpit functions (such as voice control and connected services), letting you access cutting-edge automotive tech without excessive costs.
