FAQs

  • How to import used cars from China simply?
    +
    Follow this checklist：
    1.Verify Compliance: Share your country's import rules (age/emissions). We pre-check vehicles to match them.
    2.Ship Confidently: Choose FOB/CIF terms. All cars include Third-party inspection report (accident/mileage),export licenses, and cargo insurance.
    3.Sell Faster：Your inventory becomes market-ready within 4-6 weeks of arrival.
    Need a country-specific checklist? [Ask Now]
  • How to verify compliance when importing used cars from China?
    +
    Follow this 3-step verification framework:
    1.Dealer Due Diligence
    ✓ Confirm valid export license (MOFCOM-issued)
    2. Vehicle Validation
    ✓inspection report (accident/odometer check),Original VIN plate matching export certificate etc.
    3. Legal Pre-Clearance
    ✓ Confirm with local authorities
  • Why import cars, especially electric vehicles (EVs)from China?
    +
    China's used cars have significant price advantages (30%-50% lower than Japanese and other countries' vehicles of the same age), while its new energy technology demonstrates maturity through battery warranties and high-temperature-adapted range capabilities. Government-backed transparency in vehicle sourcing and support for localized after-sales networks enable rapid fulfillment of market demand for cost-effective EVs.
  • How long does vehicle export take after purchase?
    +
    Standard processing requires 3 key steps:
    1.Pre-Export Compliance (5-7 days)
    Mandatory inspection (accident/mileage verification)
    Export license issuance (MOFCOM authorized)
    2.Customs Clearance (3-5 days)
    Submit: Commercial invoice, COO, Bill of Lading
    Pay applicable export tariffs
    3.Port Logistics (5-8 days)
    Cargo insurance activation
    Container loading & vessel booking
    Total lead time: 15-20 working days (varies by port efficiency).
