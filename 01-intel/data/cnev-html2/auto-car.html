Vehicle overview

The AITO M9 2024 series offers a blend of performance, efficiency, and comfort with both Max and Ultra versions. These 6-seat SUVs are powered by dual electric motors and a 1.5T engine, delivering a total power of 365 kW and dynamic driving performance. The 42kWh battery offers a pure electric range of up to 225 km, while the 52kWh battery extends this to 275 km. The AITO M9 accelerates from 0-100 km/h in 4.9 seconds and reaches a top speed of 200 km/h. With the dual-motor AWD setup, the AITO M9 combines exceptional efficiency with robust power, ensuring a smooth and exciting ride for all types of journeys.
