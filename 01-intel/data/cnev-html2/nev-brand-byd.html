FAQ

  • Can I buy 1 electric car?

    We have no minimum order quantity requirement. Even if you purchase 1 electric car, you can enjoy our preferential discounts and convenient export services.

  • Do you have electric cars in stock?

    We will check whether there are existing cars in the warehouse based on your procurement requirements. Generally, existing electric vehicles will not have more than 6 months of inventory. If the electric vehicle inventory period exceeds 6 months, we will communicate with you and provide you with price compensation.

  • How do I ship an electric car to my country?

    Before we confirm the order, our team will communicate with you about transportation matters, including transportation methods, transportation costs, customs declaration and clearance, insurance and arrival at the port, etc. Our team will help you complete these tasks, so please feel free to buy our electric cars.

  • What are your payment methods and terms?

    We have two payment terms of FOB and CIF. We recommend that you choose CIF payment terms, because customs declaration is a very complicated process that requires a lot of export and certification information. Our operation will be faster and error-free. Our payment method supports TT, with 30% advance payment and 70% balance payment after vehicle inspection.

  • Do you provide special chargers for electric cars?

    If the electric vehicle manufacturer does not provide a charger, you will need to purchase it separately. We can help you purchase original chargers or non-original chargers.

  • Do you have the qualification to export electric vehicles?

    We are one of the first batch of electric vehicle exporters approved by the Ministry of Commerce of China and have rich export experience. If you purchase electric vehicles from other companies in China, we can provide you with export services.

  • Can I become your distributor in our country and region?

    We very much welcome powerful companies from any country and region to become our electric vehicle distributors and help us expand the local electric vehicle market.

  • Can we visit an electric car manufacturing factory in China?

    We very much welcome you to come to China to visit our company. If your electric vehicle purchase is very large, we discuss cooperation options and visit the electric vehicle manufacturing factory you would like to visit.
